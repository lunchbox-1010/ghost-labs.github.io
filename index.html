What we're about

Ghost Labs, LLC is a small studio that ships software with intention.

1

Privacy first

We use Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your devices, but it lives in your private iCloud — never on our servers. We collect as little as possible, full stop.

2

Built to evolve

Some apps are subscription-backed, others are one-time purchases. Either way, you get continuous updates — new features, polish, and fixes keep shipping long after you buy.

3

Quietly delightful

Considered design, smooth animation, and tiny touches that make the everyday a little nicer.

4

A little bit of magic

Dust pixies, hidden animations, tiny surprises. Our apps have a playful side because the everyday should feel a little brighter.
