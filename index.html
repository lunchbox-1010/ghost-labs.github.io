What we're about

Three practices, one philosophy.

1

Privacy first

We collect as little as we can. When we run things on Azure, your data stays in your tenant. Our apps lean on Apple's CloudKit so family content lives in your iCloud — never on our servers.

2

Built to evolve

We don't ship and disappear. Apps get continuous updates. Cloud platforms get hardening passes. Sites get evergreen care. Things you buy from us get better, not worse.

3

Quietly delightful

Considered design, smooth animation, and tiny touches that make the everyday a little nicer — whether you're a kid earning loot points or an ops team running a production dashboard.

4

A little bit of magic

Dust pixies, hidden animations, tiny surprises. Our work has a playful side because the everyday should feel a little brighter.
