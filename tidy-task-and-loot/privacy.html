Privacy Policy

How we handle your information when you use Tidy Task & Loot.

Overview Privacy Policy Terms of Service Support

Effective date: May 5, 2026 · Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ghost Labs, LLC ("Ghost Labs," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with the Tidy Task & Loot mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information we collect

Tidy Task & Loot is designed to be a privacy-respecting product. The information we collect falls into the following limited categories.

Information you provide

  • Task content. Tasks, lists, due dates, and notes you create are stored locally on your device. If you opt in to sync, this content is stored in your iCloud or Google account in an encrypted form and is never accessible to Ghost Labs.
  • Spending entries. Amounts, categories, dates, and notes you log on the Loot side of the App are stored locally on your device, and only synced through your iCloud or Google account if you opt in. We do not link to or read your bank, credit card, or financial accounts.
  • Support correspondence. If you email us, we keep a copy of your message and our reply so we can help you and follow up.

Information collected automatically

  • Crash and diagnostic data. If you opt in via your operating system's settings, anonymized crash logs and performance data may be shared with us through Apple or Google so we can fix bugs.
  • App Store / Play Store analytics. Apple and Google may share aggregated, non-personal install and usage statistics with us through their developer dashboards.

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, microphone data, or device identifiers beyond what is described above. We never see the contents of your tasks or spending entries.

2. How we use information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the App;
  • Diagnose and fix bugs and crashes;
  • Respond to your support requests and other communications;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your task or spending content for advertising, profiling, or any form of machine-learning training.

3. Sharing of information

We share information only in these limited situations:

  • Service providers. We use Apple's App Store, Apple iCloud, Google Play, and Google Cloud platform services to distribute the App and offer optional sync. These providers process information under their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requests. We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers. If Ghost Labs is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

4. Data retention

Local task and spending content is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Synced content is retained in your own iCloud or Google account until you delete it. Support emails are retained for up to 24 months unless a longer period is required by law.

5. Your choices and rights

  • Access and deletion. Because most data is stored locally on your device or in your own cloud account, you can access or delete it at any time directly within the App or via your iCloud / Google account settings.
  • Diagnostics opt-out. You can disable crash and analytics sharing in your device's privacy settings.
  • Email us. You can email support@ghost-labs.com at any time to request deletion of any support correspondence we hold about you.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK Data Protection Act, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), or similar legislation. We honor these rights regardless of where you live.

6. Children's privacy

Tidy Task & Loot is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. International users

Ghost Labs is based in the United States. If you use the App from outside the United States, you understand that information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those of your country.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be highlighted in the App or on this site.

9. Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email support@ghost-labs.com.
