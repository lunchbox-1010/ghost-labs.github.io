Two simple sides. One simple app.

Tasks on the orange side, money on the blue side. Switch between them with a swipe.

✓

The Task side

Quick capture for whatever pops into your head. Three lists — Today, Soon, Someday — that quietly do the organizing for you. Optional gentle reminders that never nag.

$

The Loot side

Log a spend in two taps. Watch your week and month at a glance. Set soft monthly targets per category and see where the loot is actually going — without linking a single bank account.

Why one app for both?

Because tracking what you need to do and what you spend are the two routines that benefit most from a place to dump them — and they almost always live on the same phone, in the same minute, with the same intent: "let me just get this out of my head."
