Quick answers

How do I sync between devices?

On iOS, enable iCloud sync from Settings → Sync. On Android, enable Google Drive sync. Sync is end-to-end your account — we never see your data.

Can I import data from another app?

CSV import for spending entries is on the roadmap. To-do import is currently manual — sorry, we're keeping it simple for now.

Is there a web version?

Not yet — Tidy Task & Loot is mobile-first by design.

How do I delete my data?

Tap Settings → Data → Erase all data. Or just delete the app — we don't keep anything on our servers.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by the App Store or Google Play. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com or play.google.com/store/account/orders.
