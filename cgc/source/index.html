02What we do

Selected, fabricated, installed.

Every step under one roof. We hand-pick every slab from suppliers around the world. Stone is cut and finished by our shop fabricators on Carnegie Avenue, then delivered and installed by our four installation crews — kitchens, vanities, fireplace surrounds, and commercial scope, in steady rotation.

01 — Countertops

Kitchen counters built around your cabinetry.

Granite, marble, and quartzite fabricated to your kitchen — seam-planned, edge-detailed, and sealed for the way you actually cook.

02 — Bath

Vanities, surrounds, full-height stone walls.

Wall-to-wall vanity tops, tub decks, shower thresholds, and full-height stone walls. Templated with millimeter tolerance and installed by our own crew.

03 — Selection

Granite, marble, slate, onyx, limestone, and quartzite — sourced from around the world and stocked on site so you can hand-pick the exact slab. When something out of the ordinary is called for, we go and find it.

05Our story

One shop. One family. Since 1990.

Cleveland Granite & Marble is a family-run, full-service supplier of natural stone. From our Carnegie Avenue showroom and fabrication shop, we’ve been importing, cutting, and installing stone for Cleveland homeowners, designers, builders, and architects for over thirty-five years.

We hand-pick every slab ourselves — granite, marble, slate, onyx, limestone, quartzite, and engineered quartz — from suppliers around the world. Our shop fabricators cut and finish each piece on the floor of our Carnegie Avenue shop; our four installation crews then bring it to your home and set it themselves. Most of our team has been with us for years — the same people you’ll meet in the showroom will stay with your project from selection through install.
